## Change Management in Regulated Industries

Regulated enterprises face unique challenges when automating change management. Compliance frameworks like NERC CIP, PCI-DSS, and APRA CPS 234 demand rigorous governance, audit trails, and segregation of duties.

### Topics Covered

- How compliance requirements shape change management automation
- Mapping OpsChain capabilities to specific regulatory frameworks
- Building immutable audit trails for regulatory evidence
- Case patterns from utilities, banking, and telecommunications

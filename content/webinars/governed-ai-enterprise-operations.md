## Governed AI in Enterprise Operations: From Concept to Production

AI-driven automation promises unprecedented efficiency, but enterprises need governance frameworks that ensure safety, compliance, and accountability. This webinar covers practical patterns for deploying governed AI in production operations environments.

### What You'll Learn

- How to implement AI governance without sacrificing automation speed
- Practical patterns for compliance-ready AI workflows
- Real-world examples of governed autonomous agents in enterprise operations
- The role of audit trails and explainability in AI-driven change management
